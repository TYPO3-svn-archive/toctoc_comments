Our donation system
-------------------

.. index::
	single: Donations; How to donate?

Please donate if you use this software.

We have a permanent link to our account on Paypal which allows you to make us a donation. It's on
our homepage `www.toctoc.ch <http://www.toctoc.ch/>`__. 
Donations are urgently
needed for the continuation of this work and for our support.

But we know: It's very hard to find on our homepage :-)


That's why you find a link to make a donation in the backend plugin at the bottom
of the plugin-tab.
