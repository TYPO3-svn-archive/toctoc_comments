Considerations
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The statistics at the base of top views and
therefore top activities as well, are correctly maintained from the moment on you install
toctoc_comments 4.1 or newer.

Please consider the relevant TS options


::

    advanced.countCommentViews = 1
    advanced.countViews = 1

They turn on or off the counting of
views.
